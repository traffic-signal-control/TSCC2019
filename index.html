Traffic Signal Control Competition 2019

Venue: KDD 2019 workshop

Why do we want to solve this problem?

Urban traffic congestion is a worldwide problem. Especially in a country with rapid urbanization in China, traffic is facing increasingly severe pressures and challenges. The development and application of technologies such as deep reinforcement learning, big data and cloud computing have made AI participation in urban traffic management become a reality, but there are still many spaces that can be explored and improved.

Traffic Signal Control Competition 2019, held by The Pennsylvania State University, Apex Lab from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Tianrang Inc., and Intel Inc., is aiming to let AI researchers and the public in the world pay attention to urban traffic problems and explore the potential of AI.

Timeline & Prizes

1

Online Competition

2019/04/15 - 2019/06/30

Public testing stage. The organizer will release the traffic data of five intersections. During this time period, you are expected to develop your control framework and submit the phase plan. The results of this stage will decide the final prizes of the competition.

2

Prizes & Presentation

2019/08/05

The top teams in the first stage will be invited to present their work at the KDD 2019 DIT workshop , and publish their paper at the workshop. The top teams will also be awarded a travel grant to attend the conference.
